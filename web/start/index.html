  1. Hear the orchestra

    Open the stage and tap it once — the laptop becomes the orchestra and starts playing. (Or press start here and listen on the stage tab.)

    ⚪ waiting — orchestra is idle

  2. Conduct it — no hardware wand needed

    Your hand on the webcam is a full wand. Open it, allow the camera, then pinch thumb + index and move your hand.

    🖐️ Open Webcam Wand ↗ 📱 Phone wand (HTTPS)
    ⚪ waiting — no wand connected yet

  3. Watch it react

    As you move the wand, the accompaniment changes. Big/fast → busy line · gentle → calm pad · twist → counter-melody.

    now playing: —
    Changes land on the next bar (~2 s), so move a beat ahead.

  4. Add real instruments (optional)

    On the stage, a QR code appears. Scan it with phones on the same Wi-Fi — each becomes one instrument and the laptop hands the sound to them.

    ⚪ 0 phones joined — this is optional

  5. Prefer knobs to gestures?

    The Control Room lets you drive everything by hand — start/stop, tempo, force any accompaniment, drop a MIDI song, assign instruments.
